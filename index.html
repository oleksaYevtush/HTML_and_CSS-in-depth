Gardening Solutions For You

Lucky Shrub is a garden design firm based in Tuscon, Arizona. We operate both as a design consultant and also as a shop for plants to provide what you need to transform any space into an oasis you can be proud of.

What We Do

Here at Lucky Shrub, we specialize in garden design and creation, maintenance, and landscaping. We also run a small plant nursery that sells indoor and outdoor plants. We have whatever you need to create the garden space you've been dreaming of right in your own home.

Our Staff

Lucky Shrub was started by the husband and wife team of Jason and Maria, who share a long-time love of plants. Jason is a garden architect, and he creates and oversees all the designs while managing his own team of landscapers. Maria is responsible for the marketing and oversees the nursery.
